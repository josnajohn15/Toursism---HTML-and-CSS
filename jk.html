Experience the Paradise of Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir, often called the 'Paradise on Earth,' is a breathtakingly beautiful region blessed with snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and serene lakes. The iconic Dal Lake with its houseboats and shikaras, the vibrant tulip gardens, and the ski slopes of Gulmarg make it a dream destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Explore Srinagar's Mughal gardens, experience the tranquility of Pahalgam, and indulge in the snowy adventures of Gulmarg. Rich in culture and heritage, Jammu & Kashmir is also home to spiritual sites like the Vaishno Devi Temple and the Amarnath Cave. Whether it's the vibrant spring, snowy winters, or golden autumns, every season in Jammu & Kashmir promises an unforgettable experience.
